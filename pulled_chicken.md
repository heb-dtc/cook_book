# Pull chicken

### Ingredients
* *1 kg chicken breast*
* *barbecue sauce*
* *brown sugar* 
* *two onions* 

### Directions
```
Dice the onions.
In a pot mix some oil, the bbq sauce and two spoon of brown sugar and add the onions. 
Mix the chicken with the sauce and let it cook at low fire for 5 to 6 hours.
```
